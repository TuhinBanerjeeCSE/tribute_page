Diego Maradona

Widely regarded as one of the greatest players in the history of football

Diego Maradona holding the FIFA World Cup trophy after winning the final to West Germany.

Timeline Of Maradona's Life:

  • 1960-Born in Lanús, Argentina.
  • 1976-Maradona made his professional/club debut for Argentinos Juniors.
  • 1977-Maradona made his full international debut at age 16 for Argentina, against Hungary.
  • 1979-Maradona played the FIFA World Youth Championship in Japan, emerged as the star of the tournament and also won the Golden Ball in FIFA U-20 World Cup.
  • 1981-Maradona got transfered to Boca Juniors from Argentinos Juniors(scored 115 goals in 167 appearances) for 4 million USD.
  • 1982-Maradona was transferred to Barcelona in Spain for $7.6 million.
  • 1983-Maradona(playing for Barcelona) won the Copa del Rey and the Spanish Super Cup.
  • 1984-Maradona arrived in Naples and was presented to the world media as a Napoli player.
  • 1986-Napoli led by Maradona won their first ever Serie A Italian Championship and captained the Argentine national team to victory in the 1986 World Cup in Mexico also was honoured with Golden Ball .
  • 1987-Napoli led by Maradona won Coppa Italia and Maradona was also the Top Scorer in Serie A with 15 goals.
  • 1989-Napoli won UEFA Cup under the captaincy of Maradona.
  • 1990-Napoli won Italian Supercup under the captaincy of Maradona.
  • 2000-Maradona officially retired from Club Football career.
  • 2001-Maradona officially retired from International Football career.
  • 2020-Dies at age of 60 due to Heart Attack.
